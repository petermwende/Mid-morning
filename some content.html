Kenya

Its capital city being Nairobi. Which is increasingly becoming a commercial, recreational and residential centre.Kenya carries import and export of goods to and from different parts of the world.Export of oil from Turkana, gold from Uganda coffee from many counties and tea from kericho.Also its an industrial centre flooded with industries to process their own goods.

Kenya is also a tourist attraction region due to its warm climate especially in the coastal parts, sunny beaches and its rich culture and extraordinary dishes.
